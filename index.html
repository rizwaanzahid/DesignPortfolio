About Me

I am a passionate instructional designer with extensive experience creating accessible, engaging learning experiences across in-person, blended, and online formats. My expertise lies in working closely with subject matter experts and stakeholders to develop practical, user-focused learning solutions that align with adult education principles and organizational goals. I combine creativity with structure to translate complex information into clear, effective training materials.

Instructional Approach

  • Conduct comprehensive learner needs assessments to define measurable learning objectives
  • Design and develop accessible and inclusive instructional content, including multimedia scripts, storyboards, job aids, and infographics
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams and subject matter experts to ensure alignment with operational priorities
  • Manage multiple instructional design projects from planning through evaluation using timelines, risk management, and feedback loops
  • Apply evaluation methods and analyze learner performance data to support continuous improvement of learning outcomes
  • Integrate emerging technologies, AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT), and innovative learning platforms to enhance engagement
  • Experience aligning content to standards such as SCORM, xAPI (Tin Can), and familiarity with LTI integrations
